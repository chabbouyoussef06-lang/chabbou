Profile

Born and raised in Tunisia

A Mediterranean crossroads where history shaped open minds. From Carthage’s legacy to vibrant craftsmanship and engineering talent today, Tunisia taught me curiosity, resilience, and a taste for precise, beautiful work.

First flight, first spark (2011)

I was seven, facing an Airbus A320 I never imagined so large. The engines felt monumental. Take-off scared me; cruise won me over. That mix of power and calm never left me—and it quietly set my path toward aerospace.

Making things my way

Even as a kid, I customized my toys—nothing standard for long. I liked to tweak, refine, and give parts a purpose. That habit of building and improving became my way of thinking.

Today

I’m currently based in Germany, growing through studies and early professional projects. It’s a grounded, steady journey—focused on learning, teamwork, and turning ideas into dependable aerospace work.
